What are Reindoodles?
Reindoodles is a limited collection of 1,111 reindeer doodles in the heart of Solana community. Inspired by fellow doodle art on the Ethereum chain, these cute reindeers are excited to meet their new owners.

Get those 1/1!

10 lucky community members will be able to grab a unique 1/1 to cement their status of being the alpha deers in the herd.

Reindoodles work great in pairs!

For every 2 Reindoodle held, a holder will be airdropped $REIN token which will be nifty to future team announcements. Additional details on the token will be provided in discord in upcoming days.
ROADMAP

Here's what we prepared for you!

PHASE 1

Once creating art, discord server, twitter and website is done, we will focus on building community. Giveaways will be done for whitelist or free NFT on discord and twitter. The team will make sure the project will only launch once we think the community is ready.

PHASE 2

Lucky members of the community will be able to mint 1,111 Reindoodles. We will be listing these Reindoodles on secondary markets as soon as they are sold out. Holders will be verified on discord to enter our exclusive DAO to discuss upcoming direction of the project or any alpha calls to other upcoming projects.

PHASE 3

We will take random snapshots for holders of Reindoodles. Eligible holders will be airdropped $REIN token. These tokens will serve as passes to future projects by the team. More details on this will be announced in discord on the upcoming days.

PHASE 4

Announcements of $REIN utility. We have more exciting announcements and surprises coming up. Stay tuned.
